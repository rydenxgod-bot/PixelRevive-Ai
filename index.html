About

The Free AI Image Enhancer Built for Everyone

PixelRevive AI is an open-source AI image enhancer designed to help anyone transform low-quality photos into stunning HD images. Whether you need to remove blur from image files, upscale photos for printing, or restore old photos from family albums, our neural network-powered tools deliver professional results in seconds.

Unlike traditional photo editors that require hours of manual tweaking, our AI photo enhancer automates the entire process. Trained on millions of high-quality images, our models understand what a perfect image looks like — automatically adjusting lighting, sharpening edges, reducing grain, and restoring faded colors.

Built as an open-source project, PixelRevive AI is completely free with no watermarks, no sign-ups, and no hidden limits. Developers can explore our code, contribute improvements, or build their own tools on top of our infrastructure.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PixelRevive AI.

Is PixelRevive AI really free?
Yes, 100% free forever. No watermarks, no sign-ups, no hidden limits. We're an open-source project committed to keeping AI tools accessible to everyone.
Can I remove blur from my images?
Absolutely. Our AI is specifically trained to detect and reduce motion blur, out-of-focus areas, and soft images. The HD Enhancement mode is optimized for clarity and sharpness.
How much can I upscale an image?
Our Upscale mode magnifies images up to 4x their original resolution using advanced AI that reconstructs missing details intelligently — not just simple pixel stretching.
Can I restore old family photos?
Yes! PixelRevive AI excels at restoring old, faded, or damaged photos. The enhancement engine can bring back color, clarity, and detail to decades-old family memories.
Are my uploaded images private?
Yes. We use temporary file storage that auto-deletes after processing. Your images are never stored permanently or used for training AI models.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Yes, PixelRevive AI is fully responsive and works seamlessly on iOS, Android, and desktop browsers. No app installation required.
Is the source code available?
Yes! PixelRevive AI is open source. Click the GitHub icon in the navigation to view the code, report issues, or contribute improvements.
